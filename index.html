About

We take pride in being a local nursery supplying plants native to our area, plants meant to grow and thrive locally. We also supply seasonal blooms. Looking for colorful Spring daffodils, tulips or crocus? We have got them. Looking for bright zinnias, marigolds or vinca for the hot summer months? We have many shades to choose from. We have mums and pansies for the fall and tons of evergreens for the winter.

Plants

Daffodil Image by David Underland from Pixabay

Daffodil

Daffodils are great spring flowers. They symbolize hope and new beginnings perfect for spring. There are thousands of varieties. Their sap is toxic and deters animals from eating them.

Hyacinth Image by PurpleOwl from Pixabay

Hyacinth

Hyacinths are excellent spring flowers with an intoxicating scent. They are popular early-bloomers of spring. They are beneficial for pollinators at a time when there are few blooms.

Crocus Image by Yves from Pixabay

Crocus

Crocus are some of the first flowers of spring. They often appear when snow is still on the ground. Their flowers open on sunny days and close at night or on days with little sun.

Tulip Image by Gabriele M. Reinhardt from Pixabay

Tulip

Tulips are fascinating flowers. They are one of few flowers that continue growing after being cut. Tulips symbolize love and passion and their petals are edible.
